How to join? Open the home page and click the register button. From there, you are asked to fill out the form. After you've finished, click the register button and you will be lead to your profile page. That's all!
  • Jobs Criteria
  • Jobs must not be illegal.
  • You are not allowed to post more than 10 jobs per day.
  • Do not use the jobs feature as your personal advertisement.
  • Jobs section is strictly for jobs only and not any other of your personal means.
  • Minimum Rewards
  • Must be at least be half of the minimum wage of where the job giver lives.

Jobs Workflow

Head over to the Jobs page to either make or claim a job.
To take a job, you only need to click "Accept" on the job you want to do. Keep in mind that the job will then be not available to others.
You may post a job by filling out the form below the job list and submit it.
